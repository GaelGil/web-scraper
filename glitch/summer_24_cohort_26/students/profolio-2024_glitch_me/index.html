You know me by Milly (or Milli w/ an "i"), but I officially go by Millicent Villanueva! I grew up moving around California, but mostly around the Bay Area and Sacramento region. While moving around, I had the honor to explore the design world through different forms of media and culture. Nonetheless, this lead down the creative pathway into graphic design. Additionally, I am a huge fan of interior design and animation all around (as most of my inspiration comes from furniture and anime). I find myself looking over vintage catalogs of MCM furniture and I'll always have an animation film playing while I study or work (especially Studio Ghibli). Although those are my interests, my hobbies lie heavily in arts and crafts! Particularly, I enjoy crocheting/knitting hats or scarves and altering and clothing for myself or others! Nonetheless, my creative side has always been an aspect I love to explore and share!
And hopefully I can do the same with you!
