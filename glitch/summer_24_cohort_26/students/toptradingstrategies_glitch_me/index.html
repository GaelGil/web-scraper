Why is picking the right
strategy important?

In order to become a profitable trader, It's important to choose a strategy
that fits your personality and that you'll be able to stick with for the long term.

How do I know which strategy is for me?

In order to figure out which strategy fits you best, you have to test each
strategy out for at least 3-6 months. It is recommended to test a strategy
out by taking live trades when the market is open, and reviewing how the
strategy performed in the past year or more.
