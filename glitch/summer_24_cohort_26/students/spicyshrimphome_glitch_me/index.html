The Spicy Shrimp, located in San Francisco's Chinatown. Established in 2015, our restaurant initially set out to offer an array of spicy Chinese seafood dishes, aiming to bring a unique and bold dining experience to our community. However, we quickly discovered that our neighbors are mostly from the Guangdong Province, they preferred flavors that were less fiery but equally delightful. Embracing this feedback, we transitioned our cuisine to reflect the exquisite and delicate flavors of Guangdong Province-style Chinese food. Our dedication to authenticity and quality has made us a beloved destination for both locals and visitors.
