Delah Coffee Privacy Policy

Effective Date

2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card details)

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • IP address
  • Location data (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send promotional offers and updates (with your consent)
  • Ensure the security of your account
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following scenarios:

  • With Service Providers: To facilitate payment processing, order delivery, and other essential services.
  • With Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • In Business Transfers: If we merge, sell, or transfer parts of our business, your information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no system can guarantee 100% security. Use the app at your own risk.

5. Your Choices

  • Account Settings: You can update or delete your personal information in the app’s settings.
  • Location Data: You can disable location tracking in your device settings.
  • Marketing Communications: You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link.

6. Children’s Privacy

Delah Coffee is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with the updated effective date. Continued use of the app signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
