About Us



Dev/Mission’s Spring 2024 Pre-Apprenticeship Training at Affiliate Site, Goodwill SF Bay is a best practice, 12-week technology training movement that teaches young adults skills in IoT (Internet of Things), IT Essentials (hardware and software), coding in HTML/CSS and Javascript and Critical Career Skills.

As a class, some of us came in not knowing how to code or ever assembling/disassembling a computer. Yet, we learned how to work with others, seek out support from one another, and learned valuable career skills. In this program we grew and are still continuing to grow our knowledge in the techology industry. With that said, this is our class directory website.
