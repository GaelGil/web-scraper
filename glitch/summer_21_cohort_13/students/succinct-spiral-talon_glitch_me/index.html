All About Me

My name is Jenny Chen. I am currently 16 years old. My hobbies include reading manga and watching anime! Prior to the whole pandemic I was actively involved with my dragon boat team. If you haven't heard of dragon boat it is essentially a water sport that is comprised of 20 paddler, two in each row with a total of tens rows. During race days we have a steers person, who keeps the boat straight and a drummer, someone who beats the drum to the rhythm and speed the boat is going at.
