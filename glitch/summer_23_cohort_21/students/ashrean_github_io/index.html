ABOUT ME

Hi! I am a twenty-nine-year-old mother to a silly 16-month-old son and a polydactyl cat named Beau (aka BeauBeau). My previous profession was an associate teacher at a preschool/daycare facility located in San Francisco. I also used to work as a teacher assistant for 5th grade at a catholic school in the North Bay. The teacher I was an assistant for taught the basics of coding to her 5th-grade students and I saw how much they enjoyed it and how much I enjoyed learning with them. I want to create programs built for elementary/middle school students who have a passion or just even interested in software engineering, especially for underfunded schools.
