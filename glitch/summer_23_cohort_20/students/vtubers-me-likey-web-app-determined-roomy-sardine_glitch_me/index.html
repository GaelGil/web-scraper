Vtubers, Me Likey!

What is this website about? Like it says in the title, this website is about vtubers that I like and support!

What are Vtubers? The "V" means Virtual and "tuber" means Youtuber. Vtubers are a type of online personality who uses an avatar drawn by artists and rigged to move according to the livers movements in real time when they stream content on platforms such as YouTube and Twitch. They could make content such as cooking, playing games, tier lists and much more!
