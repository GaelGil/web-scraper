Clariza Bernabe


Hello, my name is Clariza Bernabe. I was never the type to produce my own art such as painting, sketching or music. But I considered myself a creative person due to my interest in analyzing and a connoisseur of art more so specifically architect (home and cathedral churches), stained glass, and graphic designs. Throughout my childhood and adolescent I was in a dilemma of whether or not to pursue art as a hobby since I had no motive to producing my own art. I thought in order to be an artist you need the physical artistic skill to produce your own work. I eventually stopped exercising my creative side up until these past couple years.

I began visiting local museums: Van Gogh exhibit, Asian Art museum, SFMOMA, de Young, etc. My visits to these museums made me realize that art comes in different forms and not just the surface form of art such as painting etc. When looking at graphics in a video game, I instantly get attached to itâ€™s visual content and wonder of its design process. I enjoy graphic design and interested in manipulating different approaches to producing graphic art. Aside from graphic design I am also really interested in designing a functional website. Being a very meticulous person and having analytic skills I like to think I can also fit into the role of a user experience researcher.
