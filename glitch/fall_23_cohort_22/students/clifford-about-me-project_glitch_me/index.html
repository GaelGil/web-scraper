About Me

Hello, my name is Clifford Stites and I work as an Audio Visual technician at SF State. I was born and raised in San Francisco and from my parents I learned the value of hard work, diligence, and punctuality. My passion is working with the physical components of computers or the hardware of devices in general. It was also what I loved most doing in technical theater, which was fixing lights or the circuits after each show. I am at Dev/Mission because I want to improve my IT knowledge so I can further advance my ability to repair computers for others.
