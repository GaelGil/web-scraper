Thanks for stopping by!

Ever since my childhood, I've always been curious about how everything works. Something about things from dessembling and reassembling pens to reading about da Vinci's inventions tickled my brain. I eventually pursued a degree in Biology and Asian Studies so I can learn more life's diversity processes and mechanisms. During an ecology course, I had my first introduction to programming and my curiosity became more technology-oriented. Now, I'm looking to explore different ways I can create and contribute to technology that makes a positive impact!

When I'm not creating components on Figma as a freelance UX designer, I'm helping build a community of UX professionals as an Event Coordinator of IterateUX. During my free time, you can find me hiking somewhere across the Golden Gate Bridge, reverse-engineering baked goods I've had before, or upcycling apparel from thrifted clothing!
